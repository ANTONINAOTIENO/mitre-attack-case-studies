# Snowflake Customer Data Breaches (2024)

## Incident Summary

In 2024, multiple organizations experienced data breaches involving their Snowflake environments.

Public reporting indicated that attackers used valid credentials to access customer accounts. Many compromised accounts did not have multi-factor authentication (MFA) enabled.

After gaining access, the attackers searched for valuable data and exfiltrated information from affected environments.

The incidents demonstrated the risks associated with credential theft, lack of MFA, and insufficient monitoring of account activity.
