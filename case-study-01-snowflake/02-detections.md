# Detection Opportunities

## Suspicious Login Activity

Potential indicators:

- Multiple failed login attempts
- Login from unusual geographic locations
- Impossible travel events
- Login outside normal working hours
- Login from previously unseen devices

## Suspicious Discovery Activity

Potential indicators:

- Repeated access attempts to unauthorized resources
- Enumeration of databases and datasets
- Access to resources outside the user's normal role

## Suspicious Data Access

Potential indicators:

- Large volume data queries
- Access to sensitive datasets
- Unusual download activity
